Personal Statement

My graphic design work exists as a parallel creative outlet to my architectural pursuits. Through digital illustration, I explore themes of identity, emotion, and the human experience in ways that complement and inform my spatial design thinking.

Working primarily in Procreate and Adobe Illustrator, I've developed a visual language that balances bold composition with nuanced detail. This collection represents commissioned album artwork, personal explorations, and reflective pieces created over the past five years.

Album Artwork

Commissioned pieces for independent musicians, including cover art for "Salt Water" by Josh Moreira, "Nike Airs" and "Fingertips" album artwork. Each project involves close collaboration with the artist to visually translate their sonic vision.

Personal Work

Personal pieces like "I Miss Being Alive" — a reflection on the COVID-19 period — and "Faceless Explorer" explore themes of identity, introspection, and the search for meaning. These works serve as visual journals, documenting personal growth and perspective shifts.

Design Philosophy

I believe in design that evokes emotion first and explanation second. Whether creating for clients or myself, I strive to create images that resonate on a visceral level, inviting viewers to bring their own interpretations to the work.
