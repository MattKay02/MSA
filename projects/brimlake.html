Project Statement

Brim Lake is a residential design exploration drawing inspiration from mid-century modernism. The project emphasizes the relationship between interior and exterior spaces, using large glazing panels to frame lake views and bring natural light deep into the living areas.

The design celebrates clean lines, honest materials, and a harmonious integration with the landscape. The cantilevered forms and horizontal emphasis create a sense of floating above the water, connecting inhabitants to the serene lakeside environment.

Design Approach

The structural approach prioritizes views and natural light while maintaining privacy where needed. The material palette consists of warm wood tones, exposed concrete, and expansive glass â€” a nod to the masters of mid-century residential architecture.

Interior Spaces

Interior perspectives reveal the flow between spaces, with the kitchen and living room opening seamlessly to outdoor terraces. The double-height spaces create drama while maintaining human scale through careful detailing and furniture placement.
